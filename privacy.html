Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

AVAP ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use AVAP IPTV Player ("the App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • IPTV playlist URLs (M3U links) - stored locally on your device
  • Xtream Codes credentials - stored locally on your device
  • App preferences and settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device identifier (for subscription verification)
  • Purchase history (managed by app stores)
  • Basic app usage analytics (crash reports, performance data)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Your IPTV credentials, preferences, and watch history are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store this information on our servers.

Security: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With your consent

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

For users in the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our legal basis for processing your data includes:
